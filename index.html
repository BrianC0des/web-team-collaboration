About Us

The College of Computing Studies (CCS) is one of the colleges in Western Mindanao State University (WMSU) that offers various degree programs in the field of computing and information technology. The college aims to provide quality education and training to students who are interested in pursuing careers in the rapidly evolving field of computing.

This website aims to serve the college of computing studies, help it's students through their work, announcements and to serve the community.

Courses


Computer Science

The Computer Science program at the College of Computing Studies (CCS) is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in computer science principles and practices. The program covers a wide range of topics, including programming, algorithms, data structures, computer architecture, software engineering, and database systems.

Students in the Computer Science program will have the opportunity to develop their skills in programming languages such as Java, Python, C++, and others. They will also learn how to design and implement software applications, work with databases, and develop web applications.



Information Technology

The Information Technology program at the College of Computing Studies (CCS) is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of information technology concepts and practices. The program covers a wide range of topics, including computer networks, database management, web development, cybersecurity, and IT project management.

Students will have the opportunity to develop their skills in network administration, database design and management, web development, and cybersecurity. They will also learn to manage IT projects and work with various IT tools and technologies.



Application Development

The Application Development program at the College of Computing Studies (CCS) is designed to provide students with the skills and knowledge needed to develop software applications for various platforms, including desktop, web, and mobile. The program covers a wide range of topics, including programming languages, software development methodologies, user interface design, and software testing.

Students in the Application Development program will have the opportunity to develop their skills in programming languages such as Java, Python, C#, and others. They will also learn how to design and implement software applications, work with databases, and develop web and mobile applications.
